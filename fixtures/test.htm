      The 15 Best Places for Burgers in Baltimore

      Created by Foursquare Lists       • Published On: July 30, 2019
      Share
      Tweet

      1. Abbey Burger Bistro

      8.7
      1041 Marshall St (at Cross St.), Baltimore, MD
      Burger Joint · Federal Hill - Montgomery · 150 tips and reviews

      Candice Coolahan: Build your own burger with a ballizion toppings & crazy meat (this month includes deer, duck, & llama)! & be sure to try the Alligator Bites & Crab Tots!

      Paymon: Great burgers. Loved the Santa Fe burger

      Greg S.: Had a standard med. rare Black & Blue burger; cooked PERFECTLY. Well seasoned, great all around. Best burger in the city.

      2. Kooper's Tavern

      8.6
      1702 Thames St (Btw Broadway & S Ann), Baltimore, MD
      Burger Joint · Fells Point · 90 tips and reviews

      Gene Taylor: Tuesday is burger day, Otis burger or the bison burger!! enjoy Koopers oyster fest too, November i believe. Great friendly staff too.

      Jim Eichinger: Great burgers! They also have monthly meats, such as venison or elk. Bison burgers are on the menu as well!

      Rachel L: Wonderful truffle fries and burgers. And they have Harpoon UFO white on tap!

      3. Hamilton Tavern

      8.8
      5517 Harford Rd (at Wisteria Ave), Baltimore, MD
      Gastropub · Northeastern Baltimore · 77 tips and reviews

      Lisa S.: Get the Crosstown Burger with the fried egg and bacon, substitute the onion rings ( way better) for the fries. Monday nights are burger Nights- $13/Burger,ORings AND a beer on tap. Had Flying Dog Tabl

      Obi Okobi: Veggie burger with cheddar and bacon...amazing. Don't forget the salted caramel cupcake, with bacon!

      Aaron Parker: Pretty much anything on the menu is outstanding. As good as the burgers are, don't miss a chance to go with one of the daily specials. All the food is fresh and much of it local.

      4. Shake Shack

      8.6
      400 E Pratt St, Baltimore, MD
      Burger Joint · Inner Harbor · 35 tips and reviews

      Keisha Grant: The burger was very tasty/flavorful for take out! What a great surprise. Loved that it was Angus. The burger was on the small side but I got a single. So get a double if you're feeling hungry

      Calico Mezzirro: Burgers are good, hot dogs are good, fries are amazing, shakes are tasty, extremely clean, staff was very friendly and professional. Seemed like a great environment

      Calico Mezzirro: Burgers great, hot dogs great, fries where amazing, shakes were tasty, staff was very friendly and professional, very fun and happy environment.

      5. Twist Fells Point

      9.1
      723 S Broadway, Baltimore, MD
      Moroccan Restaurant · Fells Point · 260 tips and reviews

      Angel Kino: Rack of lamb , Greek burger , braised lamb shank , byob

      Antoinette Savonero: Romantic , first date , burgers ,brunch , byob

      Deborah Cook: Best burger selection , best ambiance , service and location

      6. Abbey Burger Bistro

      8.8
      811 S Broadway (btwn Lancaster & Thames), Baltimore, MD
      Bistro · Fells Point · 14 tips and reviews

      Don Hood: Everything is good here! Wings, burgers, and chilly is to die for!!

      Andrew: Check out interesting meats on the build-a-burger list, but always top your burger with bacon and peanut butter. Trust me.

      Greg S.: 40 craft beers on tap and arguably the best burgers in Baltimore. Famous for using odd meats like camel, alligator, venison, duck and antelope... to name a few. Everything is always cooked perfectly.

      7. The Dizz

      8.2
      300 W 30th St (at Remington Ave), Baltimore, MD
      American Restaurant · Remington · 51 tips and reviews

      BaltimoreGal: Great burgers, and a good crabcake (especially for the price)

      Meredith Gould: Great burgers. Try the black and "blew" bacon burger.

      Zee: Try the black bean burger and fried sampler!

      8. Red Star Bar & Grill

      8.5
      906 S Wolfe St (at Thames St.), Baltimore, MD
      American Restaurant · Fells Point · 66 tips and reviews

      Wes Linda: The bbq burger wrap is a must and the garlic fries are amazing!

      Jamie Lynn: Get the bison burger with 1/2 and 1/2 fries (half garlic, half sweet potato)!!!

      Jon Murphy: Best bison burger in town

      9. Clark Burger

      8.6
      5904 York Rd, Baltimore, MD
      Burger Joint · Northern Baltimore · 18 tips and reviews

      Rachel L: Awesome Poutine (disco fries). Also try some of their burgers.

      Colin Kendall: Burgers, poutine and sauce game on point. Truffle Mayo = 👌 ... Highly recommend.

      Marc Mayer: I haven't had poutine this good since I lived in Canada. Add a burger and a Dude Milkshake (pretty much the best shake I've had in forever), and you're one happy kid.

      10. Of Love & Regret

      8.6
      1028 S Conkling St (at O'Donnell St.), Baltimore, MD
      Gastropub · Canton · 60 tips and reviews

      Christina: Try the French Burger is excellent. Wonderful combo of Brie, brandy poached pear, and grilled onions

      Carolyn J.: The berber burger is amazing!!!

      Chad: Burgers are rockin, Bar staff is awesome. We'll be back for sure.

      11. Smaltimore

      8.2
      2522 Fait Ave (at Rose St.), Baltimore, MD
      American Restaurant · Canton · 29 tips and reviews

      Greg S.: Nachos were surprisingly good. Great list of beers on tap. $6 burgers are priced appropriately.

      Jimmy Kastner: The stock market concept of beer keeps you drinking. Good thing they have a great beer selection. The burgers are awesome too.

      Gregg Sternberg: Great Wings....love the burgers and the grilled cheese...great beer selection and service

      12. Golden West Cafe

      8.6
      1105 W 36th St (at Hickory Ave), Baltimore, MD
      American Restaurant · Hampden · 165 tips and reviews

      Dave Fishman: Had excellent service on Halloween (Wednesday night). Excellent food too: tots app (huge, great sauces), BBQ burger (tangy!) w/ sesame noodles, carne enchiladas.

      Ambular Illig: Take the plunge and Get the burger with apple and Brie on it...it's amazing! Even the pickiest of eaters will love it :)

      Noah Sakin: The black bean burger is phenomenal! Get it with sweet potato fries!

      13. On the Hill Cafe

      7.8
      1431 John St (Mosher), Baltimore, MD
      Coffee Shop · Bolton Hill · 51 tips and reviews

      Danielle Walton: Try the smokey burger or half a sandwich with salad!

      Dana Chang: The Smokey Burger is incredible.

      Brian Mitchell: Essentially a MICA cafe with awesome burgers and sandwiches

      14. City Cafe

      8.6
      1001 Cathedral St (at W Eager St), Baltimore, MD
      Lounge · Mid-Town Belvedere · 116 tips and reviews

      Marj Mariano: The Lobster Mac and Cheese and Lamb Burger Entree is amazing. Also, drink a Get it! Berry Martini to kick off a night out. If you get there early (before 8pm) for Happy Hour, there's $5 martinis.

      Katie M.: Great brunch spot! Mimosas are inexpensive and poured in a tall glass & the Oyster Rockefeller omelette along with the Hangover burger can't be beat. Oh and don't forget the free mini Cinnamon rolls!

      Vino50: Grape American Road Trip: Try the Hangover Burger - Hangover burger with sausage. Beef and over easy egg. I didnt have a hang over but it sure was good!

      15. B&O American Brasserie

      8.3
      2 N Charles St (at Baltimore St), Baltimore, MD
      New American Restaurant · Downtown Baltimore · 73 tips and reviews

      Danielle Rossi: The B&O "power lunch" was awesome! Perfectly dressed salad, amazing burger w bleu cheese and bacon jam, fries and cookies for $14. Saving the cookies for dessert tonight!

      hana pestle: the power lunch with the burger BLEW MY MIND. one of the best burgers I've ever had.

      Brea Ellis: Happy hour burger is delicious!
